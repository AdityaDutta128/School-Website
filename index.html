Step By Step School, Noida

Our mission is to provide a caring, healthy and challenging environment where children prepare to meet with confidence, imagination and integrity the challenges of life. In partnership with supportive families we strive to develop in each child the desire and discipline to grow in wisdom, to lead lives of personal honour, to embrace diversity, to appreciate beauty, to pursue physical well being and to interact with others with a generous and compassionate spirit.

school achievements

Shiv Nadar school (Noida) MUN

SBS Students participated in Shiv Nadar School (Noida) MUN held over three days 2nd-4th August, 2018. Aryaman Dewan and Ujas Acharya were awarded a High Commendation in their respective committees.

Vijayant Thapar Memorial Inter School U-17 Football Tournament

Our U – 17 Boys Football Team secured first position in the XII Capt. Vijayant Thapar Memorial Inter School Under - 17 Football Tournament held at Army Public School, Noida from 30th October to 3rd November, 2018. Player of the tournament – Mehul Sapra
